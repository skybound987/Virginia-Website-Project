    Top 5 Facts About Newport News

  1. According to the U.S. census, the city of Newport News has an estimated population of 184,306 people.[7]

  2. Newport News was incorporated as a city in 1896.[8]

  3. It is located the southeastern tidewater region of Virginia in historic Warwick County.[9]

  4. Based on population size and high amount of industry, Newport News is an urban metropolitan area.[10]

  5. The average income level in Newport News is $57,463, compared to the rest of the state of Virginia at $80,615.[7]
