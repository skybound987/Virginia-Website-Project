Learn More About Richmond

  • According to the U.S. census, the city of Richmond has an estimated population of 229,395 people.[3]

  • Richmond was incorporated as a city in 1842.[4]

  • Richmond is located in the middle-eastern region of Virginia between the counties of Chesterfield and Henrico.[5]

  • The city of Richmond is an urban metropolitan area with much commerce.[5]

  • The average income level in Richmond is $54,795, compared to the rest of the state of Virginia at $80,615.[3]
