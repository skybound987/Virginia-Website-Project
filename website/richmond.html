WELCOME TO THE CAPITAL CITY OF RICHMOND

The cityscape of Richmond, Virginia.[3]

  • According to the U.S. census, the city of Richmond has an estimated population of 229,395 people.[4]

  • Richmond was established in 1742, incorporated as a town in 1782, and finally incorporated as a city in 1842. Richmond is located in the middle-eastern region of Virginia between the counties of Chesterfield and Henrico.[5]

  • The city of Richmond is mostly an urban metropolitan area with much commerce, with some outlying suburban areas.[6]

  • From 2017 to 2021, the average income level in Richmond was $54,795 compared to the rest of the state of Virginia at $80,615.[4]
