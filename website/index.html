WELCOME TO VIRGINIA

The borders of Virginia.[1]

Virginia is a southeastern state and one of the 13 original colonies. Virginia is one of the oldest states and is also called the Old Dominion. The historical aspects are often what attracts tourists to come visit Virginia.[2]

The webpages contained within this site explore the various aspects of several cities within Virginia, including the capital city Richmond, Newport News, and Smithfield.
