Learn More About Norfolk

According to the U.S. census, the city of Norfolk has an estimated population of 232,995 people.[12]

Norfolk was incorporated as a city in 1845.[13]

It is located in the southereastern Tidewater region of Virginia.[14]

Norfolk is also a port and part of an urban complex with adjacent cities.[14]

The average income level in Norfolk is $56,244, compared to the rest of the state of Virginia at $80,615.[12]
